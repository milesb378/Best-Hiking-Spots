Best hikes in the Amman area

Everyone in Jordan knows about Wadi Rum, Petra, Aqaba, the Dead Sea, Jerash and the other famous tourist hotspots. However, there is so much more to Jordan than these few spots. There are wadi hikes all along the dead sea and there are nature reserves that are fun for a getaway weekend. This website will be focusing on short to medium length hikes that can be done in a day.
